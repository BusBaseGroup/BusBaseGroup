Disclaimer & Contact Information

Important information about BusBase and official LynxBus customer service enquiries.

BusBase is Independent

BusBase is an independent enthusiast-created website built for fleet information, route references, service updates and transport enthusiasts.

BusBase is not officially affiliated with LynxBus, nor is it operated, managed, endorsed or supported by LynxBus.

Any references to LynxBus, fleet vehicles, services, routes or operational information are displayed for informational and enthusiast purposes only.

Contact LynxBus Directly For Official Enquiries

Please contact LynxBus directly for:

  • Lost property
  • Ticket or fare issues
  • Refund requests
  • Complaints
  • Official timetable enquiries
  • Accessibility assistance
  • Service disruptions
  • Recruitment enquiries
  • Business partnerships

BusBase cannot recover lost property, process ticket refunds, investigate complaints, or provide official LynxBus customer service.

Things BusBase Can Help With

BusBase can help with general enthusiast and information-based queries such as:

  • Fleet list information
  • Vehicle identification
  • Bus registration lookups
  • Route information shown on BusBase
  • Upcoming changes listed on the website
  • Website bugs or incorrect information
  • Suggestions for new features
  • General enthusiast discussions about LynxBus vehicles and services

Information Accuracy

BusBase aims to keep fleet and route information as accurate as possible, however LynxBus services, vehicle allocations, fleet movements and operational details may change at any time.

If you are travelling, always check the official LynxBus website before making your journey.
