Confirmed Changes

Lynx has confirmed that they are no longer serving The Buttlands in Wells-next-the-Sea. They have also announced the new route 44 "The Loop", operating Monday–Saturday, looping via King's Lynn Bus Station, South Wootton, Knights Hill, Queen Elizabeth Hospital, and Hardwick. This starts on 1 June 2026.
